1. PROJECT CHARTER
================================================================================

1.1 Project Title
    AI-Powered Sales Forecasting Dashboard

1.2 Project Objective
    Build an enterprise-grade Business Intelligence and Machine Learning
    solution that transforms raw retail sales data into actionable business
    insights and predictive sales forecasts.

1.3 Business Problem
    The Global Superstore operates across 147 countries with 7 distinct
    markets. Without data-driven insights, the business faces:
    
    - Inability to predict future sales trends
    - No visibility into regional profitability differences
    - Unknown customer behavior patterns and churn risk
    - Lack of product performance optimization
    - Inefficient inventory and supply chain decisions
    - Missed revenue opportunities from poor discount strategies

1.4 Business Value
    This project delivers:
    
    [1] Revenue Optimization     — Identify top-performing products & regions
    [2] Cost Reduction           — Spot unprofitable segments and products
    [3] Customer Retention       — Predict at-risk customers via RFM analysis
    [4] Demand Forecasting       — Predict next 12 months of sales
    [5] Strategic Decision Making — Executive dashboard for C-level stakeholders

1.5 Stakeholders
    - Executive Leadership (CEO, CFO)     → Strategic decisions
    - Sales & Marketing Team              → Revenue growth
    - Supply Chain & Operations           → Inventory planning
    - Data Analytics Team                 → Ongoing insights
    - Finance Team                        → Budgeting & forecasting

1.6 Project Timeline

    Phase  | Description                        | Duration
    -------+------------------------------------+----------
    1      | Business Understanding             | Week 1
    2      | Data Preparation & Star Schema     | Week 1-2
    3      | SQL Business Analytics             | Week 2-3

    5      | Executive Power BI Dashboard       | Week 4-5
    6      | Python Advanced Analytics          | Week 5-6
    7      | ML Sales Forecasting               | Week 6-7
    8      | Streamlit Deployment               | Week 7-8

1.7 Success Criteria
    - 50+ SQL analytical queries with documented insights
    - 5-page Power BI executive dashboard
    - Customer segmentation with 4+ distinct clusters
    - Sales forecast model with MAPE < 15%
    - Deployed Streamlit web application
    - Complete GitHub portfolio documentation

3. KPI DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

3.1 Primary KPIs

    KPI Name           | Formula                            | Target
    -------------------+------------------------------------+--------
    Total Revenue      | SUM(Sales)                         | Track
    Total Profit       | SUM(Profit)                        | Track
    Profit Margin %    | (Total Profit / Total Revenue)×100 | > 10%
    Total Orders       | COUNT(DISTINCT Order ID)           | Track
    Avg Order Value    | Total Revenue / Total Orders       | Track
    Customer Count     | COUNT(DISTINCT Customer ID)        | Track

3.2 Growth KPIs

    KPI Name           | Formula                            | Target
    -------------------+------------------------------------+--------
    YoY Revenue Growth | (Current - Previous) / Previous    | > 5%
    MoM Revenue Growth | (Current Month - Prev) / Prev      | Track
    New Customer Rate  | New Customers / Total Customers     | Track
    Repeat Buy Rate    | Repeat Buyers / Total Customers     | > 30%

3.3 Operational KPIs

    KPI Name           | Formula                            | Target
    -------------------+------------------------------------+--------
    Avg Shipping Days  | AVG(Ship Date - Order Date)        | < 5 days
    Discount Rate      | AVG(Discount)                      | < 20%
    Return Rate        | Negative Profit Orders / Total      | < 25%
    Revenue/Unit       | Total Revenue / Total Quantity       | Track

3.4 Forecasting KPIs

    KPI Name           | Formula                            | Target
    -------------------+------------------------------------+--------
    Forecast Accuracy  | 1 - MAPE                           | > 85%
    R² Score           | Model R-squared                     | > 0.80
    MAE                | Mean Absolute Error                 | Minimize
    RMSE               | Root Mean Squared Error             | Minimize

5. STAR SCHEMA DESIGN
================================================================================

5.1 Architecture: Star Schema

    The data warehouse follows a star schema pattern with one central
    fact table surrounded by four dimension tables.

5.2 Fact Table: fact_sales

    Contains transactional measures:
    - Sales (Revenue)
    - Profit
    - Quantity
    - Discount
    - Shipping Cost
    - Profit Margin (derived)
    - Revenue Per Unit (derived)
    - Shipping Days (derived)

5.3 Dimension Tables

    dim_customer  → Customer demographics & segmentation
    dim_product   → Product catalog & category hierarchy
    dim_region    → Geographic hierarchy (Market → Region → Country → City)
    dim_date      → Calendar & fiscal date attributes

5.4 Schema Diagram

                          dim_customer
                               |
        dim_product ---  fact_sales  --- dim_region
                               |
                           dim_date

5.5 Relationship Summary

    Relationship                                    | Cardinality
    ------------------------------------------------+----------------
    fact_sales.customer_key → dim_customer           | Many : One
    fact_sales.product_key  → dim_product            | Many : One
    fact_sales.region_key   → dim_region             | Many : One
    fact_sales.date_key     → dim_date               | Many : One
